Uninstallation of WIRIS plugin 2.1 for Moodle

  1. Go to your moodle and modify filters and turn off WIRIS filter:
    • Moodle 1.7 and greater Administration > Modules > Filters:
      Moodle 1.6 Administration > Configuration > Filters
    • Moodle 1.5 Administration > Configuration > Filters
  2. Restore your moodle/lib/weblib.php. In manually instalation you have backuped it, maybe on moodle/lib/weblib.php.old
  3. Remove moodle/filter/wiris directory
  4. Remove moodle/pluginwiris/ directory
